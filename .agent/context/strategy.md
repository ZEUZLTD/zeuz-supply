# ZEUZ Strategic Business Model

## 1. Core Opportunity
Position ZEUZ as the "Scalable Wholesaler" for the UK/EU 21700 battery market.
*   **Gap**: Mid-tier quantities (4-50 units).
*   **Target**: DIY Prosumers & Pack Builders.
*   **USP**: Bulk pricing without high MOQs (Â£70+), faster domestic shipping than NKON.

## 2. Competitive Strategy (Price Matrix)
*   **Retail Anchor**: Higher single-unit price (protects margin).
*   **Hero Tier (Qty 4)**: Massive reduction to incentivize basket building.
*   **Wholesale (Qty 25)**: Aggressive undercutting of UK rivals (Fogstar, Cell Supply).

## 3. Key Logistics
*   **1-12 Cells**: Royal Mail Tracked 48 Large Letter (PIP Box Hack).
*   **13-25 Cells**: Royal Mail Tracked 48 Small Parcel (Under 2kg).
*   **Bulk**: UPS Standard (30kg Hard Limit for ADR SP188 exemptions).

## 4. Compliance (UN3480)
*   **SoC**: <30% charge.
*   **Packaging**: Double-walled for bulk, 1.2m drop test compliant.
*   **Labels**: UN3480 Lithium Battery Mark required.
